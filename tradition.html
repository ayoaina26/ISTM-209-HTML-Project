"Seeing thousands of Aggies come together for the purpose of others at The Big Event Texas A&M University puts a big smile of my face. Our students' collective efforts today exemplify the spirit of service and community that defines Aggies."

- General Mark A. Welsh III, USAF (Ret.), 27th President of Texas A&M University

The Big Event is the largest one-day, student-run service project in the nation where thousands of students come together to say "Thank You" to the residents of Bryan and College Station. For the past 40 years, Aggies have participated in this annual event to show their appreciation to the surrounding community, completing service projects such as yard work, window washing, and painting for residents in need.


When and Where?
  1. Bryan-College Station, Texas
  2. Held at every Spring in mid-late March

Who?
  1. Students, Faculty and Employees of the Texas A&M System.
  2. Members of the Bryan-College Station Community.
