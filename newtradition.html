The Aggie Leadership Summit

Introducing the Aggie Leadership Summit (ALS), a new tradition that will bring together students from all walks of life to learn from each other and grow as leaders. This event will feature keynote speakers, breakout sessions, and networking opportunities to help students develop the skills they need to succeed in their careers and make a positive impact on the world. It will be held on the first weekend of March on the campus of Texas A&M University. ALS will be on par with all the amazing leadership opportunities that this school has to offer, to include the Student Conference on National Affairs (SCONA), the Intentional Leadership Conference (ILC), and the MSC Fall Leadership Conference. We hope and believe that the ALS will become a staple of the Texas A&M experience for years to come!
